hypertext remix is a browser-sequencer hacked together by kellyn loehr and lara warman

instructions:

  1. click start
  2. adjust the scrollbar in the individual browsers to your desired speed
  3. click "set speed"
  4. click the triangle to play
  5. reset browsers or quit the sequencer using the control panel in the corner
